Showcase your Booth Junkie Social galleries on your Wordpress enabled website.

== Description ==
This plug will allow you to grab all of the social galleries created with your Booth Junkie powered photobooth, and display them on your wordpress pages.
Each event will be shown on the page, with the first photo from each event shown as a thumbnail.

You can download a copy of Booth Junkie from [here](https://www.boothjunkie.co.uk/download/ "Booth Junkie Download")


== Installation ==    
= Admin Installer via search =
1. Visit the Add New plugin screen and search for "Booth Junkie Gallery".
1. Click the "Install Now" button.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Navigate to "Booth Junkie Gallery" on the Menu.
1. Enter your Licence Key(s), which can be found in the Options tab of then Admin Panel on your Booth Junkie software by clicking/tapping the top right corner of the photobooth screen.
1. Use the shortcode [BoothJunkieGallery] to display your albums.

= Admin Installer via zip =
1. Visit the Add New plugin screen and click the "Upload Plugin" button.
1. Click the "Browse…" button and select zip file from your computer.
1. Click "Install Now" button.
1. Once done uploading, activate Booth Junkie Gallery.
1. Navigate to "Booth Junkie Gallery" on the Menu.
1. Enter your Licence Key(s), which can be found in the Options tab of then Admin Panel on your Booth Junkie software by clicking/tapping the top right corner of the photobooth screen.
1. Use the shortcode [BoothJunkieGallery] to display your albums.

= Manual =
1. Upload the "booth-junkie-gallery" folder to the plugins directory in your WordPress installation.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Navigate to "Booth Junkie Gallery" on the Menu.
1. Enter your Licence Key(s), which can be found in the Options tab of then Admin Panel on your Booth Junkie software by clicking/tapping the top right corner of the photobooth screen.
1. Use the shortcode [BoothJunkieGallery] to display your albums.

That's it! Now all of the photos fom your photobooth events will automatically display wherever you use the shortcode [BoothJunkieGallery] in WordPress.
